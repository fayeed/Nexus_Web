Heirarchy maintained through the network

Heirarchy is maintained throughout the private network.. while giving freedom to the base user and also putting some user some kind of authority over the user base user.This can be done through providing Ids to the user.

  

Easy to use and implement in organisation

Registering for the private network is fast and fluent and doesn't take less than 5 minute and getting started with app also doesn't take less than 1 minute. It also doesn't require user education

  

Standard social media features

Social media features provided in the app are standard and have been piched to ease the flow of social interaction between the hierarchy of users. Some example like tweets, discussion, threads, News and chat.

  

Securing the private data

The data that is transfered through the other social media can be easily acquired and published on the other platforms. This can be avoided by using Nexus which separate every organisation efficiently and secured.

  

Highly scalable

Nexus is highly scalable and scale according to your need.It only require a single click in order to scale number of users can be manipulated efficiently
